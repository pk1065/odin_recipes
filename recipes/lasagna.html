Lasagna

Homemade lasagna just like your momma used to make

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  2. ¾ pound lean ground beef
  3. ½ cup minced onion
  4. 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  5. 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  6. 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  7. 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  8. ½ cup water
